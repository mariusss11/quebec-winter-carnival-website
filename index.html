Meet Bonhomme Carnaval!

The Bonhomme is the official representative of the Quebec Winter Carnival. He is a giant snowman wearing a red tuque and a traditional arrow sash (*ceinture fléchée*). When you book our "Grand Carnival" package, you get a themed character visit that brings the joy and "Joie de Vivre" of Quebec to your guests!

What makes it special?

The Carnaval de Québec is more than just cold weather—it's about the warmth of the community. From the Ceinture Fléchée (Arrow Sash) to the Caribou drink and the world-famous Ice Canoe Race, the carnival represents survival and celebration in the heart of winter.

  • The Ice Palace: Every year, a new glowing castle is built for Bonhomme.
  • The Night Parades: Giant floats and dancers light up the snowy streets.
  • Snow Sculptures: Artists from around the world compete in giant snow carving.
